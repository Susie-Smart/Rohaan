    Community Outreach

    “Seeing the students that I had been teaching, now having no access to school, wasn’t acceptable to me. I will find a solution…”




    I set up a unique program to teach the violin to the students of the Laxmi Nagar school, through an NGO called Akanksha. First, I ran a fund raiser for violins; then when covid struck, I helped the families of these students, with essentials and medicines. Later when school reopened (and was virtual due to covid) I lead their digital divide program to raise funds and procure 280 tablets for the school. I created content for the students in the form of a career talk series - helping them to have exposure to different careers. Finally, I have started a small investment fund, which I am managing, and the corpus will go towards continued music lessons for the students.
